Title: Breaking Major Karazalstan Data Leak

Page 1
Karazalstan has just announced that their government databases have been [b]Compromised[/b]. Thousands of files have been revealed this morning, from possible invasion plans to experimental combat aircraft designs.

The most shocking discovery was a file filled with Karazalstan's security codes, with some codes that are possibly still in use today.

Our sources are still decrypting the information, but a pattern has already emerged: all codes start with the 3 digits 3 1 7.
