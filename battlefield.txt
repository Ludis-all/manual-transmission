[b]Did You Know?[b/]

Engineers always write things down! Working with explosives can be dangerous work, so a good engineer always remembers the [b]Size of a Bomb's Payload![/b]

In Belmari, they always write down the explosion size (kilotons) in the last 4 digits of their codes!
