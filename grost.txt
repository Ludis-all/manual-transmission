[b]PM Carlson - A Step Towards Peace[/b]

[i]We have invited nations of the Eastern Bloc to participate in a temporary ceasefire. This war will prove costly and bloody, we must stop the bloodshed before the fog of war obscures our path towards peace.[/i]

[i]As a sign of good faith, the Belmari Republic has removed troops away from contested areas. We expect to see rapid progress in peace talks with Otechestva in the coming weeks.[/i]
