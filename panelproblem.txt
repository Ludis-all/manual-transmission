Title: The Panel Problem


Page 1
With the Belmari Republic and Otechestva caught up in the Titanium race, we find the nations turning everything into Titanium. From pots and pans to planes and submarines, multiple key advancements have driven these countries to make everything with this super-material.

While their titanium frenzy takes the world by storm, they still have a firm grasp on manufacturing rights. The industrial juggernauts Belmari Forgeworks and the Eastern Mantle Shipyards find their goods being shipped to every corner of the globe.

It comes as no surprise that nations like Vesperra and Karazalstan have used their products without proper verification...
